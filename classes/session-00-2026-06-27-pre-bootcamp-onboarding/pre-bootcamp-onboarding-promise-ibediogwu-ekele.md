## Key Concepts I Learned

- **Bootcamp Structure and Expectations**: The structure of the bootcamp was explained by the cordinators of the bootcamp. Success depends on consistency over intensity, active participation, and maintaining a high standard of professionalism, integrity, and respect
- **Accountability and Attendance**: Strict policies were established regarding attendance, assignment submission, and communication. Missing sessions or failing to submit assignments without prior notification results in removal from the cohort.
- **Curriculum and Learning Path**: The bootcamp is an intermediate-to-advanced level course based on the SC-500 certification. It covers identity, governance, storage, networking, AI security, and security operations. Mentees are encouraged to review foundational materials (SC-900/SC-300) from previous cohorts to prepare.
- **GitHub Workflow for Assignments**: Participants will use GitHub as their primary assignment portal. The workflow involves forking the official repository, editing files, and submitting work via pull requests to be reviewed by mentors.
- **Azure Environment Management**: Mentees will be provided with Azure accounts for hands-on labs. A critical skill taught is resource management—specifically creating and properly deleting resources to optimize the monthly $100 credit provided.
-- **Collaboration**: A dedicated WhatsApp group serves as the primary channel for communication, networking, and peer-to-peer support
